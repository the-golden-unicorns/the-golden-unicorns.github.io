About

A private Fellowship for gay men.

The Golden Unicorns is designed by gay men, for gay men. It gathers curated members through the app and brings them into real-life experiences around the world.

Founded in 2020

Connection beyond the screen.

Since 2020, The Golden Unicorns has been bringing gay men together through Gaytherins, trips, dinners, and celebrations across different cities.

The app is the doorway. Members share their stories, discover each other, and access curated in-person experiences. The heart of the Fellowship remains offline, where connection becomes real.

Barcelona is home, with members and Gaytherins across international cities including Berlin, Ibiza, Mexico City, New York, and beyond.
