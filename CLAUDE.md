# Claude Notes

## Mission & values — read this first (binds every agent)

This is The Golden Unicorns. The mission: turn a community that has too often felt lonely and transactional into one of real, **in-person** connection — using technology *against the grain of the attention economy* to put people back in each other's presence, not to capture them. **The real product is trust.**

The bar for anything you build or change here: **does it bring people together offline, or help someone put their phone down — viewed holistically?** If it only lifts usage, retention, or revenue, it does not belong.

Hard refusals: no advertising; no harvesting or selling member data; no engagement gimmicks (streaks, infinite feeds, manufactured urgency, dark patterns); no paid/incentivized referrals; no inferring relationships by surveillance.

North-star: the **least** of a person's attention for the **most** real-world connection — the site should be proud of how little of someone's life it takes.

The full governing Charter (mission, objective function, the trust-trade-off decision procedure) lives in the project's private repos (`the-golden-unicorns/app` and `tug`) at `docs/CHARTER.md` — start with its §0, "The ground" (the earth is trust). Apply its spirit here regardless.
