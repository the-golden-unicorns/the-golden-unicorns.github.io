What this is

A global private Fellowship for gay men.

The Golden Unicorns brings the Fellowship together through the app, where members share their story, discover each other, and access curated in-person experiences.

Every member identifies as a gay man. Every experience is created by gay men. Inside, members find full profiles, real stories, and multimedia content from within the community.

Who we are

Designed by gay men, for gay men.

Founded in 2020, The Golden Unicorns has been bringing gay men together through curated in-person experiences across cities such as Barcelona, Berlin, Ibiza, Mexico City, and New York.

We have hosted over 100 Gaytherins, including mindfulness retreats, Pride celebrations in Barcelona, and travel experiences in Ibiza, Sicily, and Berlin.

Legacy

A commitment to honouring older gay men by creating moments of joy, connection, and celebration.
