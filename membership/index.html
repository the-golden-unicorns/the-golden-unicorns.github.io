For The Golden Unicorns, creating a fellowship and knowing who we are as members — as well as what we contribute to the world through our mission and work — is of utmost importance.

Gaytherin' is our curated gathering for gay men: a safe, intentional space where artists, professionals, and leaders meet, exchange, and connect. These gatherings are not simply events; they are rituals of belonging, designed to strengthen a rare fellowship of driven and inspiring men.

When you attend a Gaytherin', you do not only join a moment — you step into a global circle that celebrates authenticity, creativity, and meaningful connection.

Gaytherins have taken place across the world, from Barcelona and New York to Mexico City and beyond, each one unique yet united by the same golden essence.

Is The Golden Unicorns an app?

The Golden Unicorns is first and foremost a fellowship. The app serves as a gateway to manage membership and access, yet the true heart of our blessing lives offline — in ceremonies, gatherings, and journeys across the world.

Who can join The Golden Unicorns?

The fellowship is curated for gay men who cherish authenticity, art, ritual, and meaningful connection. Admission is selective to preserve the integrity of the circle.

What does membership include?

Membership grants access to private events, curated journeys, members-only content within the app, and a global fellowship of inspiring men who share aligned values.

How do I apply for membership?

Request access through our app. Selected applicants receive a symbolic Golden Key to complete onboarding and choose their plan.

What experiences do members enjoy?

From intimate rituals and curated travel to Pride celebrations and transformative encounters, every experience is designed to be profound, joyful, and unforgettable.

Where do events take place?

Our home is in Europe, though Unicorns gather far beyond. Each event is intimate, crafted for depth and genuine connection.

Why the name The Golden Unicorns?

The unicorn is a mythic creature symbolising freedom, rarity, and truth. The golden horn embodies vision beyond the obvious — a third eye guiding us towards legacy and profound connection.
