The Fellowship

The Golden Unicorns is a private fellowship for gay men who value depth, aesthetics, and real connection. We bring together artists, professionals, founders, lovers of beauty, and men who want to belong to something more deliberate than the usual social feed.

The project lives across several expressions: memberships, legacy, the app, and Gaytherins. Each part supports the same purpose: helping meaningful men find each other, stay close, and gather beautifully in real life.

Is The Golden Unicorns an app?

No. The Golden Unicorns is first and foremost a fellowship. The app is the gateway that supports access, profiles, and continuity between real gatherings.

Who can join The Golden Unicorns?

The fellowship is curated for gay men who value authenticity, art, ritual, and meaningful connection. Admission is selective so the circle can stay intentional.

How do I apply for membership?

You request access through our application flow. Selected applicants receive their Golden Key and choose the membership path that fits their geography and way of belonging.

What do members receive?

Members receive access to the app, private invitations, Gaytherins, curated opportunities for connection, and entry into a global fellowship built around depth rather than noise.

What is the difference between Golden and Global?

Golden speaks to places where the Fellowship can be lived with more local density. Global speaks to belonging across borders, with the same care and a more distributed rhythm.

What is a Gaytherin?

A Gaytherin is a curated gathering for gay men under The Golden Unicorns: social, aesthetic, intimate, and designed to create real resonance between the people in the room.

Why the name The Golden Unicorns?

The unicorn symbolises rarity, freedom, and inner truth. The golden horn speaks to vision, artistry, and the courage to live with beauty and self-knowledge.
