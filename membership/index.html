The Fellowship

A private fellowship for gay men who value depth, aesthetics, and real connection — lived through Legacy, Gaytherins around the world, the app, and its memberships.

Artists, creators, founders, and lovers of beauty gather here to belong to something deliberate, curated, and real. Everything in the Fellowship exists to bring men closer in real life: to help them find each other, stay close, and gather beautifully beyond the screen.

Enter the Fellowship
Is The Golden Unicorns an app?

No. The Golden Unicorns is first and foremost a fellowship. The app is its online home — open only to accepted Unicorns — while Gaytherins are where that belonging becomes real offline.

What is a Gaytherin’?

A Gaytherin’ is a curated gathering for gay men under The Golden Unicorns: social, aesthetic, intimate, and designed to create real resonance between the people in the room.

Is this a dating app?

The Golden Unicorns is a fellowship. Connection here begins with recognition — at real tables, through shared beauty and conversation. Whatever grows from that belongs to each Unicorn.

Who can join The Golden Unicorns?

The fellowship is curated for gay men who value authenticity, art, ritual, and meaningful connection. Admission is selective so the circle can stay intentional.

How do I join?

You begin with our online form: tell us your story and choose the membership that fits your geography and way of belonging. Selected applicants receive their Golden Key — the invitation to download the app, log in, and take their place in the fellowship.

What do members receive?

Members receive access to the app, private invitations, Gaytherins, curated opportunities for connection, and entry into a global fellowship built around depth.

What is the difference between the memberships?

Global Unicorn is the doorway: full access to the fellowship inside the app, plus Gaytherins around the world. Golden Unicorn adds recognition: priority at Private Gaytherins, entry to Unplugged Gaytherins, and the Unicorn Insignia in solid 18ct gold. The Golden Unicorn Experience adds celebration: selected experiences worldwide and eleven personalised gestures across the year, including The Spices Ceremony.

What is The Spices Ceremony?

The Spices Ceremony is our signature ritual: a celebration of one life, told through the spices of the world — their stories, their symbolism, their fire. It is created for the celebrated Unicorn’s birthday: him and ten guests of his choosing, eleven at the table, anywhere in the world, on a date set together. Reserved for The Golden Unicorn Experience.

Is my privacy protected?

Discretion is a founding value. The fellowship is private, membership is never public, and what happens in a Gaytherin’ belongs to those in the room. The app operates under GDPR, and your story is shared only as far as you choose.

Why the name The Golden Unicorns?

The unicorn symbolises rarity, freedom, and inner truth. The horn is the crown of an awakened eye — sensitivity lived as vision, artistry, and self-knowledge. And gold, because that is where we come from.
