Manifesto

You know who you are.

The Golden Unicorns was created for gay men who move with intention, curiosity, and awareness - a private Fellowship where individuality is honoured and connection is made real.

Intrinsic Magic

Every person carries a gift that is not earned by performance. The Fellowship exists to notice it, respect it, and let it breathe.

Harmony

The Golden Unicorns gathers men who seek balance: between presence and mystery, privacy and openness, selfhood and community.

Bonds of Connection

Connection is not a transaction. It is built through shared presence, conversation, care, and moments that continue after the room has emptied.

Vision

To create a global Fellowship where gay men can find each other with intention, gather in person, and build a way of belonging that unfolds beyond the screen.

Mission

To curate members, stories, and experiences with care - through the app, through Gaytherins, and through the living Legacy of the Fellowship.
