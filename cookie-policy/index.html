Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how the public website of The Golden Unicorns Legacy SL uses cookies and similar technologies. Questions should be sent to oracle@thegoldenunicorns.com.

What We Use

The public website uses a small amount of first-party analytics to understand page views and meaningful calls to action, such as entry into the application flow. This helps us understand whether the public site is working, without using advertising trackers.

Identifiers

If you accept analytics, we store a session identifier and an anonymous identifier in your browser so we can distinguish one visit from another and understand broad navigation patterns across the site.

Your Choice

Analytics on the public site are optional. They do not load unless you accept them. If you decline, the site still works normally.

How To Change Your Mind

You can clear site data from your browser at any time. If you need help withdrawing consent, write to oracle@thegoldenunicorns.com.

More Information

For broader information about how we handle personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.
