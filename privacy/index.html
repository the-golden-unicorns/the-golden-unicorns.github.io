Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Golden Unicorns collects, uses, and protects information shared through this website, our contact channels, and our related membership flows.

What We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, social handle, city, country, and any details you include when contacting us or applying for membership.

We also collect basic website usage information, such as page views and CTA clicks, to understand how the website is being used and how visitors move through the public experience.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, review membership applications, manage invitations and events, improve the website experience, and support the operation of The Golden Unicorns fellowship.

Payments

Payment details used during membership or billing flows are processed by our payment providers. We do not store full card numbers on this public website.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers that help us operate the website, membership flows, email communications, analytics, or payment systems, and only to the extent needed for those services.

Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the fellowship, process applications, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, and protect the integrity of our services.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. If you are a member of the app, you may also have self-service account deletion options available inside the product.

Third-Party Services

This website and related flows may rely on third-party services for hosting, analytics, communications, payments, and application handling. Those providers process data according to their own privacy and security practices.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or wish to make a request regarding your data, contact us at oracle@thegoldenunicorns.com.
