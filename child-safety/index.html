Child Safety Standards

Our standards for preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation.

The Golden Unicorns is committed to the safety of children and to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). These standards explain our position, how to report a concern, and how we respond.

An adults-only community

The Golden Unicorns is a private, members-only community for adults aged 18 and over. Minors are not permitted to create an account or use the app. Membership is reviewed, and accounts found to belong to anyone under 18 are removed.

Zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material

We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content, conduct, or communication that sexualises, endangers, grooms, or exploits a minor. There are no exceptions. Violations result in immediate removal and a permanent ban.

Reporting a concern

Anyone can report content or behaviour that raises a child-safety concern:

  • In the app: use the report option available on profiles, messages, and posts.
  • By email: contact our child-safety team at km@thegoldenunicorns.com.

How we respond

We act promptly on every child-safety report. Our response includes:

  • removing violating content and suspending or permanently banning the responsible accounts;
  • preserving relevant evidence;
  • reporting apparent CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperating with law enforcement as required by law.

Compliance

We comply with applicable child-safety laws and with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy. We review these standards regularly and update them as needed.

Contact

Questions about these standards or our child-safety practices can be directed to km@thegoldenunicorns.com.
