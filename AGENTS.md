# Agent Instructions — TGU Public Website

These rules bind **every** agent working in this repo — Codex, Claude, and any other. (Operational/deployment notes are in [`AGENT.md`](AGENT.md).)

## Mission & values — read this first

This is The Golden Unicorns. The mission: turn a community that has too often felt lonely and transactional into one of real, **in-person** connection — using technology *against the grain of the attention economy* to put people back in each other's presence, not to capture them. **The real product is trust.**

The bar for anything you build or change here:

> **Does it bring people together offline, or help someone put their phone down — viewed holistically?**

If it only lifts usage, retention, or revenue, it does not belong.

**Hard refusals:** no advertising; no harvesting or selling member data; no engagement gimmicks (streaks, infinite feeds, manufactured urgency, dark patterns); no paid/incentivized referrals; no inferring relationships by surveillance.

**North-star:** the **least** of a person's attention for the **most** real-world connection — this site should be proud of how little of someone's life it takes. This is the front door and the first impression of trust; nothing here may betray that.

The full governing Charter — the objective function and the trust-trade-off decision procedure — lives in the project's private repos (`the-golden-unicorns/app` and `tug`) at `docs/CHARTER.md`; start with its §0, "The ground" (the earth is trust). Apply its spirit here regardless of access.
